About TempoChat

Our Mission

Redefining Digital Communication

TempoChat was created to address the growing concerns about digital privacy and data permanence. We believe that private conversations should remain private—not stored forever on corporate servers or mined for advertising.

Security First

End-to-end encryption ensures only you and your recipients can read messages.

Auto-Cleanup

Conversations automatically disappear after your chosen retention period.

Open Source

Our code is publicly auditable, ensuring transparency and trust.
